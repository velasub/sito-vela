About Us

Our Passion? Your Journey

We are a group of friends and expert skippers, united by an unconditional love for the ocean and a deep, lifelong knowledge of the Coast's hidden wonders. Every secluded cove, every cliffside village, and every local flavor is part of our personal history — and we want to share that authentic beauty with you.

Nicola

Expert Skipper & Federal Sailing Instructor

A former surveyor who followed his true calling. Nicola delights in guiding guests through hidden wonders, sharing his deep knowledge of local culture, Mediterranean cuisine, and the finest regional wines.

Gianluca

Engineer & Passionate Sailor · 20+ years

With the precision of an engineer and the soul of a sailor, Gianluca brings meticulous care to both navigation and vessel maintenance, ensuring the highest standards of safety and comfort.

Vincenzo

Construction Executive & Dreamer at Sea

Whenever he can leave the building sites behind, Vincenzo escapes to the sea. His passion is contagious — he brings the same leadership from his professional life to the deck, ensuring every guest feels the magic of the sailing dream.
